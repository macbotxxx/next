About Next Cash And Carry

The Next Cash And Carry Market is an events center ofering the charm of a vintage barn with the accommodations of a fresh built space. Featuring Bride & Groom quarters, restrooms, and a warming kitchen for outside caterers, we are prepared for all of your event needs.

We are breaking ground on The Next Cash And Carry Market in May 2020 and are anticipating a Grand Opening in Fall 2020. The Farmhouse Barn land is available for weddings and events before the Grand Opening.

The Story.

Since launching in 2009, Next Cash And Carry has empowered over 11,000 small scale-farmers across worldwide. We are committed to expanding our reach in order to continue to empower local farmers, positively impact their lives and their families while boosting food production and sustainability.
